Scoring rubric — how to assign 1–10
  • Price & €/m² (35%) — Compare €/m² to the Amstelveen 2-bed median and to WOZ. Asking at/below WOZ and below median €/m² ≈ 8–10; well above ≈ 1–3.
  • Legal / risk (25%) — Eigen grond or erfpacht afgekocht, healthy VvE with solid reserve, no monument/dispute ≈ 8–10. Running erfpacht with near-term revision, thin VvE reserve, or unclear history ≈ 1–4.
  • Distance to Emmakade 33 (15%) — ≤8 min bike ≈ 9–10, ~15 min ≈ 6, >20 min ≈ 1–3.
  • Energy label (15%) — A/B ≈ 9–10. Lower label but cheap, easy upgrade (e.g. C→A with insulation/HR++) counts as a plus, bump +1–2 vs raw label.
  • Distance to Zuidas (10%) — ≤15 min bike/transit ≈ 9–10, ~25 min ≈ 6, >35 ≈ 1–3.
  • Renovation (deduction) — Score condition 1–10 (move-in ready ≈ 8–10; cosmetic updating ≈ 5–6; major modernisation ≈ 1–4). Applied as a penalty: deduction = max(0, 6 − score) × 0.3, so decent/move-in-ready places lose nothing and fixers lose up to ~1.5 pts.
